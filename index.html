Tabbouleh Salad Recipe

How to make a tasty Tabbouleh Salad!

Ingredients

  • 3 Cups Parsley, finely chopped
  • 1/3 Cup Mint, finely chopped
  • 2/3 cup Bulgur Wheat
  • 1 English Cucumber, diced
  • 1 large Tomato, diced
  • 2 Scallions, chopped
  • 1 Garlic clove, grated
  • Cinnamon, pinch
  • 3 Tablespoons Lemon Juice
  • 1/3 Cup Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Sea Salt

Instructions

  1. Get a large mixing bowl
  2. Whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, salt, coriander, and cinnamon.
  3. Add the parsley, cucumber, tomato, bulgur, mint, and scallions and toss to combine.
  4. Season to taste and chill until ready to serve.
